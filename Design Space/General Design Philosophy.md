
# Card Design

## Book of Lenses
#### Lens of Problem
- What situation is this for? What problem does it solve?

#### Lens of Decision
- What choice does this create? What options do you have?

#### Lens of Tradeoff
- Why would you not take this option?

#### Lens of Economy
- What is being exchanged by making this choice?

#### Example
Card:
	Cost: 1; Draw 2.
Problem:
	Cannot find key cards
Decision:
	Spend energy finding cards or working with what you have?
Tradeoff:
	No immediate effect.
Resource:
	energy -> Card access

## Emergent
#### Complex Behavior created by simple rules interacting

Simple clear rules and mechanics that combine to make interesting interactions

#### Amplification
One system increases another.
- Draw -> find more draw ->snowball
- block -> converts to damage -> scaling

#### Conversion
One resource becomes another.
- Block -> Damage.
- Hp -> energy
- Status -> Effect

#### Trigger Chains
Events cause other events
- On draw -> deal damage
- On damage -> draw
- on block -> apply status

#### Conditional Loops
Conditions that feed themselves
- Gain strength when hitting
- hit more because you have strength
