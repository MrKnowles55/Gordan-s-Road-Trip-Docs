
## Round Flow

- Each round follows the [[Round Structure]]
- After the end of the round it cycles back to the beginning starting a new round

## Action Resolution

- All [[Action]]s go through the [[Action Queue]]
- [[Action]]s are anything that modify the game stat such as damage, block, draw, etc.

## Card Play Rules
- [[Card]]s are the primary way for the player to interact in the Combat System
- [[Card]]s generate [[Action]]s that affect both the player and enemies
- [[Card]]s follow the [[Card Play Pipeline]]
